    Horror Games Some horror I recommend

    Phasmophobia

    Phasmophobia is a game where the player tries to find out the type of ghost in a certain location. It's super fun to play with friends because you work together to try not to die. You can upgrade your ghost hunting equipment to help your investigations.

    Lethal Company

    Lethal company is a game where, you are a contracted worker for the Company. Your job is to collect scrap from abandoned, industrialized moons to meet the Company's profit quota. These dangers prey upon the vulnerable and lonesome, and the protection of your crew may be your only hope.

    Content Warning

    Content Warning is a co-op game where you film spooky stuff with your friends and try to go viral. Get famous or die trying!
